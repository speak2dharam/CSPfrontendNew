Flexible subscription plans to fit your business needs

Windows 365 AHB (Azure Hybrid Benefit) is a revolutionary cloud computing solution that allows businesses to harness the power of the cloud while maximizing cost savings. Discover the key benefits of Windows 365 AHB and learn how it can streamline your organization's IT operations, enhance productivity, and reduce costs.

  • Optimize Your Cloud Investment
    Maximise Savings with Azure Hybrid Benefit

    By leveraging your existing on-premises Windows Server and SQL Server licenses, you can reduce the cost of running your workloads in the cloud using Windows 365 AHB. As a result, you can save up to 40% on virtual machines and up to 55% on SQL Server instances, optimizing your cloud investment and maximizing your cost efficiency.

  • Seamless Integration and Migration
    Streamline Your Transition to the Cloud

    Windows 365 AHB simplifies migrating your existing workloads to the cloud by offering seamless integration with your on-premises infrastructure. This tool ensures a smooth transition, minimizing downtime and disruption while allowing you to take advantage of the cloud's scalability, flexibility, and security.

  • Enhanced Security and Compliance
    Protect Your Data and Meet Industry Standards

    Windows 365 AHB incorporates advanced security features, such as encryption, multi-factor authentication (MFA), and threat protection, to safeguard your data and applications. Moreover, Windows 365 AHB complies with industry standards, ensuring your organization meets security and compliance requirements.

  • Simplified IT Management
    Centralize Your IT Operations

    Windows 365 AHB streamlines your IT management by providing a centralized platform for managing your cloud and on-premises infrastructure. As a result, your IT team can focus on more strategic tasks rather than managing multiple environments.

  • Scalability and Customization
    Tailor Your Cloud Experience to Your Needs

    Windows 365 AHB offers a range of subscription plans and configurations, allowing you to scale your cloud resources according to your organization's unique requirements. Choose from various packages that include different processing power, storage, and memory levels to ensure optimal performance for your workloads.
